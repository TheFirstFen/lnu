# Example Document

Einstein's famous equation is $E = mc^2$.

## Block Math
The integral of a function can be expressed as:
$$
\int_a^b f(x) \, dx = F(b) - F(a)
$$

## Matrix Example
A 2x2 matrix looks like this:
$$
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{bmatrix}
$$

## Chemical Equation
Water formation can be written as:
$$
\ce{2H2 + O2 -> 2H2O}
$$
